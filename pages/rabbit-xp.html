Information on average costs associated with caring for your rabbit(s).

Hay, pellets, greens, litter, and toys cost $30-$60 per month.

Your rabbit(s) will need a wellness check-up every year at the vet which will cost $75-$125.

The average cost of a vet visit for a sick rabbit is $400 and surgery can cost more than $1200.
