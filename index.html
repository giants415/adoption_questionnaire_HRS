Thank you for your interest in adopting from House Rabbit Society!

To reduce the number of rabbits that are returned, House Rabbit Society wants to make sure you (and your family) are prepared to take care of your new rabbit. To get started, follow the 3 steps below

1

Watch all of the videos below (approximately 30 minutes) to learn about caring for a rabbit in your home

2

Fill out the HRS Adoption Questionnaire

3

Set up a time to visit HRS and review your application with an adoption counselor
