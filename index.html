Thank you for your interest in adopting a rabbit from House Rabbit Society!

House Rabbit Society wants to make sure you (and your family) are prepared to take care of your new rabbit. This helps reduce the number of rabbits that are returned and also keep spots open for us to continue rescuing rabbits around the Bay Area.

Please watch through all of the videos below (approximately 30 minutes) before starting the adoption questionnaire. These videos will help you and others in your home learn about caring for a rabbit.

When you are finished, please fill out the adoption questionnaire by clicking the "Start Adoption Questionnaire" button at the bottom of the page.
