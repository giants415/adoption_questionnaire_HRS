Welcome!

Thank you for your interest in adopting a rabbit from House Rabbit Society!

Our goal is to make sure that you (and your family) are prepared for the responsibilties of bringing a rabbit into your home. This helps HRS reduce the number of rabbits that are returned to us which also helps us keep more spots open to take in new rabbits!

To get started, please watch through all of the videos below. These videos will help you and others in your home learn about caring for a rabbit in your home! After you finish, please fill out the adoption questionnaire by clicking the "Start Adoption" button below.
